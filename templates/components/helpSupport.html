Metadata Review Support

Accurate and comprehensive metadata is critical for ensuring the integrity, discoverability, and compliance of your content assets. Our Metadata Review process is designed to maintain the highest standards of data quality across your portfolio.

What is Metadata Review?

Metadata Review refers to the systematic validation and verification of descriptive, administrative, and technical metadata associated with your digital assets. This ensures metadata is:

  • Accurate, complete, and consistent
  • Aligned with organizational standards and industry best practices
  • Compliant with applicable legal, licensing, and regulatory requirements

Reviewed elements include: titles, descriptions, keywords, rights information, dates, and identifiers.

Metadata Review Guidelines

  • Follow our metadata schema and formatting requirements
  • Include all mandatory fields marked as required
  • Avoid duplication, irrelevant entries, or inconsistent terminology
  • Verify rights and usage information
  • Maintain consistency in style, spelling, and data conventions

Refer to our Metadata Submission Standards for detailed specs.

Metadata Review Workflow

  1. Submission: Metadata is submitted via the designated portal or interface.
  2. Automated Validation: Completeness, formatting, and schema checks.
  3. Manual Review: Expert assessment of relevance and accuracy.
  4. Feedback & Resolution: Corrections requested or approval confirmed.

Turnaround Time: 24–72 business hours, subject to volume and complexity.

Common Metadata Issues

  • Incomplete or missing required fields
  • Inconsistent terminology or formatting
  • Invalid rights and licensing data
  • Localization errors or mismatches

Please check our Metadata Quality Guidelines before submitting.
